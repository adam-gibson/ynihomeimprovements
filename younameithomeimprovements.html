Plastering & Rendering

With over two decades of dedicated plastering experience, Sean delivers a standard of finish that sets him apart. Whether you need a single room re-skimmed or a full exterior render, the result is always smooth, durable, and beautifully finished.

  • Skimming over old surfaces or new plasterboard
  • Full strip and re-plaster
  • Exterior sand and cement rendering
  • Dot and dab dry lining
  • Coving and decorative plasterwork
  • Patch repairs, crack filling, and remedial work
